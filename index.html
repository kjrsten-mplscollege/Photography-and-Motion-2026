Studio Q Parking Tips

We have 3 parking spots marked ‘Studio Q’ in front of the building. Please only park in these spots, as other spaces belong to tenants. To avoid tickets or towing, you can also park for free on the street near Indeed Brewery or Monroe St. Two paid parking lots are also available within a block of the studio. If you need help finding us, just call or text Studio Q at 612-756-8131
